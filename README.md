## Proposal Templates

### 1. NSTA-NABT-Workshop-Template.md
**For**: Biology/science teacher conferences
**Format**: 90-minute hands-on workshop
**Includes**:
- Complete session outline with timing
- Learning objectives and NGSS alignment
- Materials checklist and tech requirements
- Presenter bios and supporting materials
- DEI considerations

**Example Title**: "Making Systems Thinking Tangible: Interactive Cell Models for K-12 Classrooms"

### 2. Research-Conference-Template.md
**For**: Academic conferences (AERA, ICLS, ISDC)
**Format**: 20-minute research presentation or full paper
**Includes**:
- Abstract (350 words)
- Full paper structure (25-30 pages)
- Presentation outline with slide guidance
- Methods, results, discussion framework
- References and data availability

**Example Title**: "Impact of Systems Biology Modeling on Student Understanding of Complex Biological Networks"

### 3. EdTech-Showcase-Template.md
**For**: EdTech conferences (ISTE, FETC, CoSN)
**Format**: 15-minute product demo or exhibition booth
**Includes**:
- Elevator pitch and problem statement
- Solution overview and unique value proposition
- Demo flow (15 minutes)
- Evidence of impact (testimonials, data)
- Booth design elements

**Example Title**: "ModelIt K-12 Systems Biology Platform Showcase"
